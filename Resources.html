Top Educational Resources for Sri Lankan Students

Here is a list of free educational resources tailored for Sri Lankan students, including local and international platforms that support the Sri Lankan curriculum (especially in Sinhala, Tamil, and English). These are useful for school students from Grade 1 to A/L, and even university-level learners.

Sri Lankan Curriculum-Based Resources

1. Channel NIE

  • Website: Channel NIE+1
  • Provided by: National Institute of Education (NIE)
  • Content: Modal papers,Vidoe Lessons(Tamil Medium , English Medium , Sinhala Medium)
  • Languages: Sinhala | Tamil | English

2. DP Education

  • Website: www.dpeducation.lk
  • DP Education is the education arm of the Dhammika & Priscilla Perera Foundation, a private foundation engaged in charitable work, especially in education and health.
  • Content: Free online school grade-learning (grades 1-13) in subjects like Maths, Science, English, IT, etc. Also early childhood content (pre school).
  • They have youtube channel for
    1. Maths video lessons in Sinhala Medium , Tamil Medium and English Medium
    2. Science Video Lessons
    3. English Vidoe Lessons
  • Languages: Sinhala Medium | Tamil Medium | English Medium (Offers courses in multiple languages (7+), including preparation for international language exams like IELTS, TOPIK, etc. DP Language School)

3. e-Thaksalawa (Official)

  • Website: www.e-thaksalawa.moe.gov.lk
  • Provided by: Ministry of Education (Sri Lanka)
  • Content: School textbooks, past papers, tutorials. (Grade 1 to A/L)
  • Languages: Sinhala | Tamil | English

4. Nenasa TV (Dialog TV Education)

  • TV-based education platform for Grades 6 to 11.
  • Also available online via Dialog ViU App or Dialog ViU website.
  • Created with support from the Ministry of Education.

5. MOE LMS (Learning Management System)

  • Website: https://lms.moe.gov.lk
  • Free access to digital textbooks, lessons, quizzes, and video tutorials.

International Free Learning Platforms (Useful for Sri Lankans)

These are not Sri Lanka-specific, but still excellent for supporting local students.

6. E-Kalvi

  • Website: e-kalvi.com
  • Provided by: e-Kalvi Charity Fund Inc. (formerly known as Jaffna University Graduates Association Victoria Inc.)
  • Content: model past papers, worksheets, term test papers, etc for students from Grade 1 up to G.C.E. A/L.
  • Visit their website to download past papers, model papers, marking schemes, worksheets etc.

7. Khan Academy

  • Website: www.khanacademy.org
  • Great for: Maths, Science, Computing, Economics.
  • Video-based + practice quizzes (in English)

8. British Council Sri Lanka - Learn English

  • Website: www.britishcouncil.lk/english
  • Learn English via videos, games, grammar tips.
  • Specially designed for Sri Lankan learners.

9. Past Papers Wiki (Sri Lankan Past Papers)

  • Website: www.pastpapers.wiki
  • O/L and A/L past papers.(Sinhala, Tamil, English mediums)
  • Great for exam practice.
